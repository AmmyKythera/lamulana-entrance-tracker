    Important Rando Info!

    • Nebur "Map" item is MISSABLE make sure to talk to Nebur before entering ruins (the outside talk she gives)
    • Hiner Shop opens after Fobos opens the gate next to his tent -- only first two item slots are randomized
    • Mulbruk item is after 4 bosses, Giltoriyo item (Funeral) is after 6 (will give you the Alsedana check too if you missed)
    • Fobos hands over item for a showing him a skull instantly
    • Freya will give her item whether you first talk to her in Endless or in Annwfn
    • Brokkr/Eitri (Frey Boat) need to read tablet in Kujata's room
    • If you have a better whip/shield than the item, it just won't appear (will look already open, mural will say you have it, shop will say sold out)
    • Claydoll Suit counts as an Anchor, as an Ice Cape, and the laser attack might be needed to break things or sometimes kill minibosses (often Cetus)
    • Bronze Mirror is 100 optional based on following use of the game timer seconds:
      • Ajisukitakahikone: 0-15 at Hraesvelgr gate
      • Takamagahara: 0-15 unmarked
      • Village: 16-30 unmarked
      • Icefire: 31-45 unmarked
      • Divine Fort: 45-60(0) unmarked
    • Sub-weapons only in logic if you can buy ammo
    • Ankh Jewel should be unsoftlockable (if you softlock, that's a bug!)
